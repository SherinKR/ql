Western dance - Balliamo

×

Details

Dance is an art of expression. Have you got what it takes to express and impress the panel? College of Engineering Vadakara in association with Quasso Liberum, brings the intercollege western dance competiton Make Your Move
Sync your crew and style the best of your steps.
Amaze the contest to bag whopping cash prizes worth 10k
Date : 22/03/19
Time : 4:25 PM

RULES

1. There is no restriction in seeking external help for background music.
2. The participating team will be responsible for the removal of their sets,
properties, etc, immediately after the completion of performance.
3. Judgement is based on rhythm, formation, expression, costumes, makeup, co-ordination
and overall effects.
4. Judges decision will be final.
5. Maximum number of participants for a team is 8 and minimum of 4.
6. Maximum time allotted for stage setting is 2 minutes and performance is 10 minutes.
